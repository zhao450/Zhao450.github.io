Since 2024, I have been pursuing my Master’s degree at the School of Computer and Artificial Intelligence, Lanzhou University of Technology, under the supervision of Professor Fuqing Zhao. My research interests include genetic programming, dynamic scheduling theory, reinforcement learning, and complex system modeling.
